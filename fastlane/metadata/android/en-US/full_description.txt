<p>This is a todo app with local backup and restore functionality. It has many features:</p><ul><li>Write a todo</li><li>Edit a todo</li><li>Mark todo as favourite</li><li>Mark todo as completed</li><li>Different sections for each</li><li>Create a local backup with time and date mentioned</li><li>Restore any backup from backup list on the app</li><li>Very easy and accessible features</li><li>Restore backup from a valid json file</li></ul>
